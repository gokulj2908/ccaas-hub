## Job Description
* Design, develop, and implement scalable Salesforce solutions based on business requirements.
* Develop and maintain Apex classes, triggers, Lightning Web Components (LWC), Visualforce pages, and asynchronous processes.
* Build and support integrations between Salesforce and external applications using REST/SOAP APIs and middleware tools.
* Work closely with business stakeholders, architects, admins, and cross-functional teams to deliver high-quality solutions.
* Customize and enhance Salesforce Sales Cloud and Service Cloud functionalities.
* Perform code reviews and ensure adherence to Salesforce best practices, security standards, and coding guidelines.
* Troubleshoot complex production issues and provide effective resolutions.
* Optimize application performance while managing Salesforce governor limits and platform constraints.
* Support deployment activities, release management, and CI/CD processes.
* Mentor junior developers and provide technical guidance to the team.

## Required Skills & Experience
* 4–7 years of strong Salesforce development experience.
* Expertise in:
    1. Apex Classes, Triggers, Batch Apex, Queueable, and Future Methods
    2. Lightning Web Components (LWC) and Aura Components
    3. SOQL/SOSL and Salesforce Data Modeling
    4. Salesforce Flows and Process Automation
    5. REST/SOAP APIs and third-party integrations
    6. Salesforce Security Model, Roles, Profiles, and Sharing Rules
* Experience with Git, deployment tools, and CI/CD pipelines.
* Strong understanding of Salesforce governor limits and performance optimization techniques.
* Excellent debugging, analytical, and communication skills.

## Good to Have
